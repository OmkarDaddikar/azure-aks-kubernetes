About Pilankar Udyog

Pilankar Masale has been serving spices and ready to cook instant Masale from last few decades. We are one of the leading Masale industry in Kolhapur. Legacy of Pilankar udyog started in 1960 with a small household business started by Mr. Shankar Rao Pilankar. Shankar Rao started a small business of basic Masale for his livelihood. His wife Mrs. Sudha Shankar Rao Pilankar supported in his work. Taste and quality of Pilankar Masale created a great demand in the society. Eventually with the help of his family members he moved his household production to a commercial place.

By the time his younger son Mr. Dilip Pilankar started working with him with great interest. He worked hard for the growth of Pilankar udyog. He improvised the work in many ways. In 1992 along with his wife Mrs. Sayali Dilip Pilankar. Pilankar udyog was grown to a well-known food industry in Kolhapur and near by areas.

Mrs. Sayali Pilankar a food technologist belonged to a metro city. Thus, knowing how difficult it is to manage work and cooking simultaneously. She developed a new range of Ready to cook instant Masale. Looking at her efforts and response from customers Mr. Dilip worked hard for expanding his brand all over Maharashtra. Over a period of time the couple took Pilankar udyog brand to a new level. In no time products of Pilankar Masale reached to many parts of Maharashtra as well as in few parts of Karnataka and Goa.

Maintaining the taste and quality Pilankar udyog has developed a wide variety of products. We have basic range of dry spices such as garam masala, goda masala, Kolhapuri masala (Kanda lasun chutney), Malvani masala. We have special range of chutneys. For festive purpose we have Modak pith, anarse pith, chakali Masala, chivda Masala, dudh masala. We have ready to cook instant Masale series which makes cooking easy and hastle free. It includes veg as well as non-veg variety such as misal masala, pavbhaji Masala, Butter chicken, Hyderabadi dum biryani, paneer makkhanwala, kaju Kurma and many more.
